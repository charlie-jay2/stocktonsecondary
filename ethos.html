Our Ethos

At Stockton Secondary School, we believe in fostering a culture of respect, responsibility, and resilience. We aim to provide a holistic education that not only develops academic excellence but also shapes the character and values of every student. Our ethos centers around inclusivity, community engagement, and lifelong learning.

We encourage our students to be curious, critical thinkers, and compassionate individuals who contribute positively to society. Our school community is built on the values of aspiration, care, integrity and respect.

Our Motto

sic itur ad astra

‘by the good deeds you do, you will be remembered in the stars for eternity’ Aeneid, Virgil
